suppose hamare pass input data hai user ka, to per sec ya ek sath bahut sara data jata hai jisse ham kehte hai stream of data aur stream of data ke andar bi bahut chote chote data hote hai unhe ham chunks bolte hai, suppose per sec kai sara KB data jara hai aur us KB data main 1 KB data bi hai to that 1KB data is a chunk(single)..
